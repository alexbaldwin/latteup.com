Flight Path

Does not feature live music

Writing by Taylor Loren Photographs by Andy Keil

If you’re one of those people that likes it loud, do not come here. Flightpath is best for studying types who need peace and quiet with a side of coffee, or the undergrad majoring in Philosophy major thats like to ponder in silence. One of the few places in Austin that doesn’t feature live music, Flightpath does feature coffee from Texas Coffee Traders and is filled with minimalist furniture and people working quietly while wearing yoga pants.

Outside on their dog-friendly patio, you’ll be able to grab a cold brew or a beer and sit down at their picnic tables, but watch your volume unless you want to be hushed (no, really). Flightpath also serves a variety of food, ranging from standard pastries and pre-wrapped sandwiches to breakfast tacos and pizza. If you want a break from all the quiet, head over to the “Biscuits and Groovy” food truck around the corner.
